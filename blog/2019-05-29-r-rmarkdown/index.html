A Sunny Day for Once

Written: May 22, 2019   

         Hello again, this week I decided to go check out the Hague! I have been here a few times before this, but I had never set aside for a full day to just walk around it and see what there is to do. Actually, I don’t think I had ever been there before in the daylight. So, I hopped on a train and set out to explore what turned out to be one of the coolest Dutch cities I have visited. After getting off the train, I was struck by modern buildings mixed with the more classic ones. This was a totally different sight than Rotterdam because it felt much more like the new mixed in with the old. Rotterdam, on the other hand, is the new and the newer mixed in with the single classic building that was somehow missing during the bombings in World War 2. Anyway, while in the Hague I tried to see all the important Dutch government buildings. Then, I set off to go see the Peace Palace. This is where the international court of justice is located. The building was really impressive, but they have it locked down pretty tightly, so I wasn’t able to get much closer than the outside fence. Afterwards, my friend and I set out to hike for a little while in a large park nearby. I found this especially pleasant because there are very few places in the Netherlands where you can go to just be surrounded by trees. To end the day, we relaxed in a beer garden near the center of the Hague and enjoyed what was one of the most pleasant days I have seen in a while.

         I think its probably well past the time for me to update you on my biking adventures. I still ride my bike just about ever day. I’ve gotten pretty good at not falling off and I can even ride with one hand. I could probably stand up and petal too, but I never really try because it’s so flat here. I think it is fair to say I’m pretty assimilated on the biking front. As for next week, I will be in Luxembourg hopefully having an excellent time. It should also be a bit of quiet week because there are some weird Dutch holidays that cancelled one of my important classes all week long. With any luck I’ll use the extra time productively, less for traveling and more for organizing myself. Thanks for reading!
